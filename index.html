the freitag story

In 2016, FREITAG adopted the Holacracy organizational structure. In a nutshell, it means that we distribute authority across the company in the form of roles rather than putting it in the hands of a few supervisors and managers.

customization

BAG CUSTOMIZATION

We’ve extended the production chain from the FREITAG factory to the sales front. There – at the FREITAG Yourself Stations in Grüngasse, Zurich, Bangkok, Kyoto and Munich or online with the F-Cut tool – you can put together unique truck tarp bags to suit your own taste.

EXCLUSIVE BAG MODELS

How it works: You choose the main body, handles and pouch of your bag to suit your own taste. And we take care of the rest on the sewing machine. Where: You can assemble the products at all FREITAG Yourself Stations.

FREITAG YOURSELF STATION

Doing it yourself with FREITAG: In this F-Store, you can choose the tarp sections for your F740 SONNY SY or your F741 MIAMI VICE SY and then put your one-off piece together to suit your own taste. You can do it live on site or virtually via a video call.
